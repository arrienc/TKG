The Kedar Group

We champion the spirit of possibility and the courage to transform

The Kedar Group (TKG) works with government and global corporate leaders and their teams to achieve higher levels of cohesion and effectiveness. TKG helps organizations increase alignment, engagement and collaboration through best leadership practices.

Our Approach

The Kedar Group (TKG) specializes in the tools and strategies of communicative, collaborative and global leadership intelligence — strengthening organizational relationships and in turn, enhancing team effectiveness while producing politically-savvy global leaders.
 

Organizational transformation happens when leaders recognize that each aspect of their organization is as strong as the relationships that hold them together. Organizations truly transform when leaders commit to strengthen those relationships by practicing clarity and authenticity in communication. The Kedar Group works with leaders and organizations to facilitate organizational transformation, creating clarity, trust and openness in the workplace.
 

 
Our Approach

The Kedar Group (TKG) brings a dynamic, integrative and reflective approach to executives, leaders and teams who are committed to making a difference and achieving results. We coach, facilitate and guide clients in the areas of leadership development and presence, promoting emotional agility, engagement and collaboration.

We see all organizations as the sum of conversations and the web of relationships conversations create between teams. Effective leadership is the art of cultivating and leveraging those relationships to produce robust, high-capacity teams that achieve superior results, and that’s where we come in. We work with leaders to explore the integration of relationships, resources and possibilities to accomplish lasting results through organizational transformation.

TKG introduces executives, both young and seasoned, to communicative distinctions that help them create clarity in the way they envision the future and manage their teams to deliver the best product possible.

How We Work

We employ tools and strategies of communicative, collaborative and global leadership intelligence — strengthening organizational relationships, enhancing team effectiveness and producing politically savvy global leaders.

We work closely with individuals and organizations to discover their leadership development opportunities by conduct qualitative individual and organizational assessments to determine:

  • Where our clients are now,
  • Where they want to go, and
  • What might enhance their movement forward or deter them from getting there?

We collaborate with our clients to design hands-on experiential approaches, strategies, and programs to enhance the ways they lead, work and achieve results.

Together we decide if the best approach will include:

  • Individual executive coaching,
  • Team coaching,
  • Dialogue sessions,
  • Action learning sessions,
  • Strategic planning retreats
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Trainings.

Who We Serve

We bring a dynamic, integrative and reflective approach to executives, leaders and teams who are committed to making a difference and achieving results. The Kedar Group (TKG) works with government and corporate leaders and teams, both domestically and internationally.

Aspiring Leaders

The Kedar Group guides developing professionals through their transition from individual contributors to becoming team-oriented managers and leaders.

Startups

We guide startup leaders to manifest their vision into reality by developing strong organizational processes and meaningful working and communicative relationships.

C-Suite Executives

When working with organization-wide leaders, TKG brings already successful executives to their next level of excellence by helping them further enhance their capacity and effectiveness as individual leaders, allowing them to lead their team to achieve superior results.

Public Service

We support government leaders and their teams to maximize the potential in their work environments and allow them to seize opportunities and identify possibilities for innovation, growth and development as they serve the public.

Global Enterprise

We help global leaders and teams cultivate collaborative solutions for complex challenges as they navigate the seas of change in a global world.

LEAH KEDAR

Leah has over 25 years of experience leading organizational change efforts worldwide. She works with clients in the U.S. and internationally, facilitating organization transformation from the inside out, helping teams, leaders and organizations in diverse organizational environments, including: government, corporate, not-for-profit, political and academic. Ms. Kedar is known for her exceptional ability to bring together disparate voices in the pursuit of common goals. She is the author of Voices of Children: AIDS Orphans in the District of Columbia and editor of Power Through Discourse. Ms. Kedar is based in Washington, D.C.
 

 
JOY LEACH

Joy has over 25 years of experience in O.D. consulting, workshop design and training, and executive coaching. She has substantial experience in designing and developing programs for diversity, management skills, team communication and collaboration. Joy is co-author of Personal Power: Today's Guide for the Working Woman, and A Practical Guide to Working With Diversity. Joy is based in Santa Barbara, California.
 

 
Libby Robinson

Libby has 20 years experience as a facilitator, change partner, and executive coach. She focuses on an integral approach to client issues with a special emphasis on how linguistics, emotional issues, and systems influence commitment and culture in an organization. Libby has special expertise in facilitation and in "real-time" coaching to improve decision making and implementation processes. She is based in Paris.
 

 
Steven Jones

Steven has 20 years experience in facilitation, executive coaching and training for organizations, focused on building more effective workplace environments. He specializes in design and development of coaching and training protocols for leadership development, team building, communication, systemic change, and diversity management. Steven is an adjunct professor at the University of Maryland. He is based in Washington, D.C.
